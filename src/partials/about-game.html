About Game

A-Puzzle-A-Day brings the acclaimed physical puzzle into the palm of your hand. Created by the original designers at DragonFjord, this digital version offers an authentic and expanded experience.

Solve today’s date every single day—with a fresh challenge and satisfying logic-based gameplay. It’s smart, smooth, and deeply rewarding. Perfect for quick breaks or your daily thinking ritual.

Every puzzle is based on a real date—you’ll never solve the same one twice. New day, new layout, new logic. It’s the perfect mix of routine and surprise.
